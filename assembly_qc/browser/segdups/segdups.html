SegDups

Description

Segmental Duplications identified with SEDEF.

Methods

Segmental Duplications were produced by SEDEF. Repeats in the assemblies were identified using TRF, RepeatMasker, and Windowmasker. The identified repeats were merged and used to softmask the assemblies and the soft masked assemblies were used as input to SEDEF. The output from SEDEF was filtered for pairwise sequence identity >90%, length > 1 kbp, and satellite content <70%.
